Custom Home Builder — Scarsdale · Larchmont Manor · Rye · Westchester County, NY

Westchester County's Premier Luxury New Construction Home Builder

For more than 20 years, Eilon Amidor has built luxury new construction homes across Scarsdale, Larchmont Manor, Rye, and Westchester County — establishing a reputation guided by permanence, precision, and an unwillingness to compromise on the details that define a truly exceptional residence.

The work begins with standards, not features.

Born in Israel — where a deep appreciation for enduring construction and refined design runs through the culture — Eilon Amidor brought that sensibility with him to Westchester County, where he has spent more than two decades establishing himself as one of the area's most respected luxury builders, with work spanning Scarsdale, Larchmont Manor, Rye, and beyond.

Specializing in custom single-family estates, Eilon is recognized for blending modern innovation with timeless architectural styles. Every project reflects his passion for superior craftsmanship, refined aesthetics, and creating residences that stand as true works of art. His name has become synonymous with prestige, discretion, and an elevated standard of luxury living.

Rather than chasing trends, the work is guided by permanence: strong proportions, refined materials, practical elegance, and homes that feel composed from the first exterior line to the final interior detail.

Design Partnership

Miguel Sostre, Architect

Eilon works in close collaboration with architect Miguel Sostre, whose portfolio of luxury residences across Westchester has established him as one of the region's most respected designers. Together, they approach each residence as a complete work — from massing and exterior character to the final interior detail.

Principles
