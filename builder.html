The Builder

A Point of View Built on Restraint, Judgment, and Enduring Quality

Eilon Amidor Homes approaches luxury development with a disciplined standard: every decision should strengthen the architecture, improve the way the home lives, and hold its value over time.

The work begins with standards, not features.

For more than 15 years, Eilon Amidor has developed luxury residences in Westchester County with a focus on architectural integrity, client discretion, and a level of finish aligned with the upper tier of the market.

Rather than chasing trends, the work is guided by permanence: strong proportions, refined materials, practical elegance, and homes that feel composed from the first exterior line to the final interior detail.

The result is a body of work created for clients who understand that true luxury is not only what is seen, but what is quietly executed throughout.
