The Builder

A Disciplined Approach to Luxury Development

For more than 20 years, Eilon Amidor has developed luxury residences throughout Westchester County, establishing a reputation built on architectural integrity, meticulous craftsmanship, and a standard of finish that defines the upper tier of the market.

Eilon works in close collaboration with architect Miguel Sostre, whose portfolio of luxury residences across Westchester has established him as one of the region's most respected designers. Together, they approach each residence as a complete work — from massing and exterior character to the final interior detail.
