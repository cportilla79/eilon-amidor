The Builder

A Disciplined Approach to Luxury Development

For more than 15 years, Eilon Amidor has developed luxury residences throughout Westchester County with a focus on quality, discretion, and architectural permanence. His work is guided by the belief that exceptional homes are not defined by excess, but by judgment — in scale, in materials, in proportion, and in execution.

The result is a body of work that serves a discerning clientele seeking homes that feel deeply considered, highly functional, and built to hold their value over time.

Custom single-family residences shaped by timeless architecture and modern livability
A detail-driven construction process with an emphasis on finish quality and architectural integrity
A discreet, highly controlled experience suited to the expectations of the ultra-luxury market
